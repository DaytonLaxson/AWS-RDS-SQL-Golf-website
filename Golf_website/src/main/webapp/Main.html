ABOUT US

Come here to hit some balls on the range and hangout with friends. The golf place has a great enviroment to chill and drink some beer. Cheap beer and a cheap bucket of balls can supply a great night for a easy price.

The buckets of beer with be cheap and this all adds up to a great place where college kids will like to go on the weekdays and the weekends.

A bucket of beer and balls is only 13usd.
